The Special Collections Department of the University of Idaho Library includes those materials that, because of subject coverage, rarity, source, condition, or form, are best handled separately from the General Collection. The several “collections” housed in this department are for research use by faculty, students, visiting scholars, and the public. However, the materials are non-circulating; their use is limited to the Reading Room.
