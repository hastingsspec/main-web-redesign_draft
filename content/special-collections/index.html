The Special Collections and Archives department specializes in the acquisition, preservation, and use of archival research materials that document the history and culture of Idaho and the University of Idaho.

Our physical material is available for use throughout the year via our Reading Room. Many of our collections can also be viewed online through the Library's Digital Collections and finding aids can be discovered in Archives West.
