Special Collections and Archives contains a vast amount of forestry related information. We are also currently building a forestry portal to better organize and present our many digital collections related to forestry resources, such as the Experimental Forest and Savenac Nursery Photo Archive, the Family Tree Collection, and the Potlatch Lumber Company Photograph Collection

Understanding Our Collections

Below is a searchable and sortable table listing collections by title, group number, and resource links. The links point to a finding aid, which is a description of the collection and what it contains. In cases where that collection has been digitized, a link to a digital collection is also included. You can also use the buttons below to limit the table to display only a specific group of collection (photo, manuscript, or university).
