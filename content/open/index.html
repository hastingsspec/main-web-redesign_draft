University of Idaho Library supports initiatives in Open Access and Open Education to make an impact across campus and the world. Join us in celebrating Open Access Week 2018 and learn more about our current projects:

Access Publishing Fund

The University of Idaho Open Access Publishing Fund (U of I - OAPF) supports U of I authors in their efforts to publish in open access journals. Funding is provided by the University of Idaho Library, Office of the Provost, and Office of Research and Economic Development to help make the innovative research conducted at U of I as widely accessible as possible.

Open Access Resolution

The faculty of the University of Idaho Library believes that open access to scholarship positively supports the communication of library research and scholarship and the future of libraries and education. To formally acknowledge our position, library faculty voted to adopt an Open Access Resolution on August 23rd, 2018.
