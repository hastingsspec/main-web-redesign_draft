Some streaming videos from Kanopy are purchased with a one year license. Access to these titles expire on a rolling basis. When planning to use a Kanopy video during the semester, please check the expiration date on this page to ensure uninterrupted access. If you would like to use a video that will be expiring soon, please contact Jodi Haire (haire@uidaho.edu) to ask about re-licensing options.
