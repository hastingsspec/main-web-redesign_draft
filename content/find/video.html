The library has an extensive collection of DVDs and streaming video for the classroom and personal use of our patrons. DVDs are found on the second floor and can be discovered in the library catalog. Please visit the DVDs page for more information and search options.

Streaming Video

The library has access to two streaming video platforms which can be accessed on campus or by logging in with your UI account:

  • Swank Digital Campus - The library has procured unlimited access to the top 250 films in Swank Film Library (most popular titles based on bookings at universities and colleges around the United States).
  • Kanopy - the library purchases one year licenses to films on demand from the Kanopy catalog. Using our mediated purchasing model, patrons will need to submit a request to the library in order to access new Kanopy videos. Requests can be submitted via the Kanopy website.

If you have questions about these services or need to view a streaming video from a provider other than Kanopy or Swank, please send a request to Jodi Haire at haire@uidaho.edu.
