Library Launches Student-Focused "My Library" Marketing Campaign to Expand Awareness of Library Services


The "My Library" campaign features timely, real-life library stories from University of Idaho students across the institution. The goal of the campaign is to highlight students and little-known library services by asking, "What does your library look like?" By sharing student experiences directly to students by students, and featuring real students and real solutions to educational needs, the campaign is changing the perception of the library.
