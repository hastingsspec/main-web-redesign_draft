Visitor Parking Options

Pay to Park

There is a mix of self-serve pay station parking and metered parking in close proximity to the Library. Both of these options allow for payment with debit/credit cards and coins. Hourly rates vary based on the location and time of day and range from $0.75 to $1.50 per hour. Consult the campus meter pay station map for exact locations of pay station and metered parking:

View Full Size

Visitor Permit

Purchase a Visitor permit to park in a regular parking space in any Gold, Red, Blue, Purple, Silver or Green parking lot or any marked Visitor space on campus. Visitor permits are $3/day and can be purchased online, or at the Parking Office located in the Intermodal Transit Center on the corner of Sweet Avenue and Railroad Street (1006 Railroad Street). Overnight Parking

Permits

An annual or temporary overnight permit may be purchased for use by those who reside off campus and need to park on campus between the hours of 2:00 a.m. and 7:45 a.m. Pricing and purchasing instructions can be found on Parking's Other Permits & Forms page.
