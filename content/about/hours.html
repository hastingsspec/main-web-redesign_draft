Special Collections Reading Room
Fall and Spring Semesters

The Special Collections and Archives Reading Room (Room 121) will be open Monday – Friday 10:00am – 2:00pm or by advance appointment during the fall semester (August 20 – December 14) and Monday – Friday 9:00am – 4:00pm during the spring semester (January 9 - May 10). Patrons can make an appointment via email or phone: (208) 885-0845 / Email: libspec@uidaho.edu.
