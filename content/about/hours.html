Current Library Hours and Reopening

The Library is following a phased reopening model as part of our ongoing response to COVID-19. Hours listed on this page are subject to change.

We continue to offer extensive services online, including our reference desk and research help with extended hours.

  • Reference Desk is online with extended hours!
  • Interlibrary Loan services are available online for digital resources.
  • The MILL, The Studio, and CDIL are closed.
