U of I librarians are available to help via chat, text, or email during Reference and Information Desk hours. Meetings with a librarian are also available by appointment during regular hours, please email libref@uidaho.edu to set up a time. Even when the Reference and Information Desk is closed, Chat help from librarians from other academic libraries is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, all year-round!

You can also contact your subject liaison or call our Circulation Desk (208-885-6559) for assistance.
