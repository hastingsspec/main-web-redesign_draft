Access Library Resources Using NetID

You will need to enter your Net ID (Vandal email address and password) when prompted to log-in to most library resources from off-campus. If you don't know your Net ID, please contact ITS via their website, email, or phone (208-855-HELP)

Net ID is used for the following resources:

  • Off-Campus Access to Databases and Ebooks
  • Requests from other Summit Libraries

Please contact the Reference Desk with any questions.

Interlibrary Loan

A library barcode number is needed for Interlibrary Loan Requests.

If you do not have a Vandal Card, please call (208) 885-6843 or email Interlibrary Loan to receive a barcode number.
