Librarians can help you efficiently and effectively search and use information using library resources as well as the Web and other electronic resources. Each College at U of I has a faculty librarian assigned as a subject liaison who is ready to help one-on-one with all your research and library questions.

To request a Zoom meeting with a librarian, please find your subject liaison below and click the button to get in touch. If you are unsure, reach out to our reference librarians and we will make sure your questions get answered.
