Local Libraries:

  • Washington State University (WSU) Libraries.
  • University of Idaho Law Library, web site and catalog.
  • VALNet, a consortium of public and school libraries in north central Idaho and southeastern Washington.
  • Latah County Library District, branches in Moscow, Bovill, Deary, Genesee, Juliaetta, Potlatch, and Troy.
  • Neill Public Library, Pullman, WA.

Idaho Libraries:

  • Boise State University Library.
  • Idaho State University Library.
  • Orbis Cascade Alliance, a consortium of 39 colleges and universities in Oregon, Washington, and Idaho.

International Libraries:

  • WorldCat, an online database that searches the catalogs of 36,000 libraries worldwide.
  • Library of Congress.
  • National Agriculture Library, access to full book catalog and Agricola article database (1985-present).
  • British Library.
