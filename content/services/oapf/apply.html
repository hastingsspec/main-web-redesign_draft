Application Process

  1. Review eligibility criteria
    • Reach out to the OAPF Fund Manager or their library liaison with any questions
  2. Submit an application
    • Upon submission, applicants will receive a confirmation email with a copy of their application

Award Process

  1. The OAPF Fund Manager will use a standard rubric to evaluate the application based on eligibility criteria and make funding decision
  2. The OAPF Fund Manager will email applicants their funding decision and a copy of the rubric
  3. Funded applicants will then email their invoice or proper documentation of payment directly to the OAPF Fund Manager and the Libraryâ€™s Fiscal Officer.

Invoices and Reimbursement

The U of I - OAPF prefers to pay journals directly, thus minimizing out-of-pocket costs to the authors. However, in the case of publication deadlines, authors may opt to be reimbursed with proper documentation of payment. Other possible payment options will be reviewed as needed, e.g. paying out of departments with intra-institutional account transfers.
