The University of Idaho Library has access to two streaming video platforms with content that can be used in the classroom or assigned to students:

  • Swank Digital Campus - unlimited access to the top 250 films in Swank Film Library (most popular titles based on bookings at universities and colleges around the United States).
  • Kanopy - the library purchases access to films on demand from the Kanopy catalog, with licenses that last one year. Patrons will need to submit a request to the library in order to access new Kanopy videos. Requests can be submitted via the Kanopy website.

If you need to view a streaming video from a provider other than Kanopy or Swank, or for more information please contact Jodi Haire or your subject liaison.
