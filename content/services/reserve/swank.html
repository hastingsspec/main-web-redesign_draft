Swank Motion Pictures - Digital Campus

The University of Idaho Library offers support for the Swank Motion Picture streaming movie service. This service offers an extensive catalog of movies and documentaries from most major Hollywood and independent movie studios for classroom use. Licensing through Swank is done on a title-by-title basis, and licenses last for one year.

For more information please contact Jodi Haire or your subject liaison.

Visit the Swank Motion Pictures webpage for more information on available titles.
