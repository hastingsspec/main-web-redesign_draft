Where do I start?

Where to start

To log-in to most library resources from off-campus, you will need to enter your Net ID at the prompt (Vandal Email address and password). If you don't know your Net ID, please contact ITS at https://help.uidaho.edu/, support@uidaho.edu, or 208-885-HELP.

Net ID will be used for the following resources:

  • Off-Campus Access to Databases
  • Off-Campus Access to Ebooks
  • Requests from other Summit Libraries

For Interlibrary Loan Requests, a library barcode number is still needed. If you do not have a Vandal Card, please call 208-885-6843 or email libill@uidaho.edu to receive a barcode number.

Please contact the Reference Desk with any questions at (208) 885-6584, libref@uidaho.edu, or via chat at http://www.lib.uidaho.edu!

The resources of University of Idaho Library are available for students both in and outside of Moscow, Idaho. If you are taking classes online or at one of the University of Idaho centers, check out our research guide.

The guide and information below will also be useful for students taking U of I classes online, or even those from Moscow who are using the library's resources while traveling or from home.
