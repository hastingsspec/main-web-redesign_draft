GIS Day 2020 @ U of I: Advancing Science and Community Through GIS and Drone Technologies is a free, virtual event that brings together scholars, students, professionals, businesses, and the public to discuss geospatial technologies and demonstrate their many uses. Admission is free and open to everyone. Please register to receive Zoom session information!

Please contact Bruce Godfrey (bgodfrey@uidaho.edu, 208-292-1407) with any questions.

Sponsor GIS Day @ University of Idaho

If you or your organization would like to become a sponsor of GIS Day at the University of Idaho please use one of the following options to make a donation:

  • Online
    Go to UIdaho Giving
    Under "Designation" type in "GIS Day Fund" and fill out the required fields of the form
  • By phone
    Call the University of Idaho Gift Administration Office at (208) 885-6796 and give with a credit card.
    Indicate you are giving to the "GIS Day Fund."
  • By mail
    Checks may be mailed to:
    University of Idaho Foundation 875 Perimeter Drive MS 3147 Moscow, ID 83843-3147
    Please have the checks made out to the "University of Idaho Foundation, Inc."
    Put "GIS Day Fund" on the check memo line or on a note accompanying the check.

Sponsorship Levels & Entitlements

We welcome any financial contribution but respectfully suggest to following amounts:

  • Friend: $50
  • Professional: $100
  • Corporate: $350 - logo on web page & flyers; banner space; recognition during the event

Please contact Bruce Godfrey (bgodfrey@uidaho.edu, ph. 208-292-1407) if you have any sponsorship questions. These contributions may be tax deductible.
